The Yellow Dry Lemon

When the lemon trees reach their period, it is then picked and taken to a special and open area to dry under the sun, after the sun exposure, it’s separated into the wanted ones and others and the cleaned and perfect ones are packed. Again, it’s repackaged as per the request from the customers. Somali Dry lemons from Somalia is characterized by its quality, as it’s purely organic, and also as it’s harvested twice a year which made its supply to be a reliable and continuous and its availability long-term storage.
